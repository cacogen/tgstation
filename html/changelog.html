26 January 2021

AdamElTablawy updated:

  • Spiders are now stuck on harm rather than help intent, meaning you can't trade places with them like other simplemobs.

ArcaneMusic updated:

  • The genetics camera now has the correct name.

Arkatos updated:

  • Changelings will now show correct ID job icon on security huds upon transformation, if ID was actually equipped as a part of the flesh disguise. Upon reabsorbing the flesh disguise ID, the icon will also immediately disappear, so keep that in mind.
  • Changeling flesh disguise IDs will now properly show overlays upon examine.
  • Changeling flesh disguise will now properly include suit storage slot item, if there is any.

Jared-Fogle updated:

  • Friendly quirk has been lowered from 4 to 2 points.

Maurukas updated:

  • Missing disposals pipe in Kilostation's robotics lab is replaced
  • Missing sci-drobe added to R&D

RaveRadbury updated:

  • Allows tobacco products, food trash, fancy boxes, organs, and a few other things onto trays.

Son-of-Space updated:

  • Standardizes KiloStation APCs
  • Delta APCs have been replaced with their superior autoname versions

SparkezelPL updated:

  • Mosins now have 10 armor penetration and lowered wound chance
  • Russian surplus now costs 50% more
  • Due to their old age and poor maintenance Mosin Nagants can jam now, use a gun maintenance supplies on them after combat to make them keep their reliability.
  • Added gun maintenance kit, it contains gun maintenance supplies you can use to make sure your mosin doesnt jam. You can find them in russian surplus crates

TemporalOroboros updated:

  • Neon carpets exists. Bug your local chemist or quartermaster for a supply.
  • Decals support user-defined planes and some forms of smoothing.
  • Emissive layers now support emissive turfs, items, and mobs as well as emissive blocking structures.
  • You can recombine stacks of carpet you split apart again. Did anyone notice that this was broken?

Timberpoes updated:

  • Noogies are now combat logged.
  • The Crew Monitor should no longer stop functioning when Doctor Hilbert hangs out in nullspace, and should continue to work despite any similar errors in the future.
  • Dense machines once again deconstuct into frames instead of all their raw component parts.

cacogen updated:

  • Painting exhibit names and descriptions are different
  • Paintings no longer tell you they're fair to middling

eeSPee updated:

  • Codex Cicatrix and Fake Pierced Reality toys.
